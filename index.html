Coquí del Yunque

Guide a Puerto Rican coquí through misty ruins, waterfall caverns, and storm canopies. Chirp open hidden routes, collect relics, and carry the chorus deeper into El Yunque.

Three Living Routes Each level now mixes canopy climbs, ruin ledges, and hidden echo paths instead of one-note stepping stones.
Smoother Power Rhythm Power blooms feel easier to grab mid-run, and attacks are meant to chain with less dead air.
Coquí-Led Traversal Chirp pads, wall vines, and moving leaf bridges push the game toward a more distinct rainforest identity.
Richer Visual Grounding Warmer coquí art, brick ruins, moss, and hanging vines give the world stronger Puerto Rican character.
